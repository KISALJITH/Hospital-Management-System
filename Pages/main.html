Our Services

Dental Clinic

We feel that anxiety about dentistry is unnecessarily common and aim to reassure you that consulting a dentist need not be feared anymore here and may even be enjoyed at the hospital Dental Care Unit.

Endoscopy/ Colonoscopy/ Gastroscopy Units

We provide acute care to both the inpatient and outpatient, as well as screening procedures for diagnostic and preventative purposes. The Endoscopy Unit provides a broad range of endoscopic services.

24x7 OPD Service

The OPD observation Unit Unit provides care for patients with conditions which can be treated without the need for an overnight stay in hospital.

Emergency Treatment Unit (ETU)

Urgent and emergency care is the range of healthcare services available to people who need medical advice, diagnosis and/or treatment quickly and unexpectedly.

24x7 Labouratary Service

Accurate and timely laboratory results can be critical to a patient’s diagnosis and treatment. Laboratary provides a full range of diagnostic testing services that are accurate , timely and cost effective for patients and the medical profession.

Pharmacy

Better Health Care through a Good Hospital Pharmacy service. Pharmacy of NMH are experts in rational drug therapy for a speedier cure and faster recovery. Come and visit

Fully Equipped Operating Theater

Medicare Hospitals operating theatres department is an innovative and forward looking department comprising of three operating theatres, covering a comprehensive range of surgical specialties.

Physiotheraphy Unit

Physiotherapy aims to help you to optimize your mobility, function and independence. Hospital strives to provide an in-patient and out-patient service that is based on high standards of care and quality. Staff work closely with medical, nursing and other allied health staff.

Vaccination For Children & Adults

This Clinic runs a vaccination program that is on par with the National Vaccination Program. Be assured of the safety of yourself and that of your child through immunization against life-threatening diseases.
